Open a property detail page from the portfolio snapshot or property directory.

## Summary Header

The header shows the property name, status, type, address, and an **Edit property** action.

## Key Metrics

Property detail pages summarize total units, occupied units, vacant units, occupancy percentage, monthly rent, active leases, open tickets, and leases expiring within 90 days.

## Units and Leases

The Units section shows a sample of units, including occupied or vacant state and the assigned tenant where lease data exists. The Leases section shows attached lease records, including unit, tenant, dates, status, monthly rent, overdue balance, email, and phone when available.

## Property Contact

If contact information was added, the Property Contact section shows contact name, email, phone, and address.

## Maintenance and Financials

Maintenance lists tickets attached to the property, including title, status, priority, and due date. Financials summarizes gross scheduled rent, overdue balance, leases expiring within 90 days, estimated vacancy loss, and owner distribution.
