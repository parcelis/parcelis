Currently all features provided by Parcelis are open-source and free to use. Long-term product backlog will eventually introduce a pricing structure to unlock Parcelis+ features.

## Is There a Cost to Sign Up for Parcelis?

Parcelis is free to sign up and use. There are no upfront costs or required subscriptions to get started at this time.
