# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Parcelis is committed to making participation in this project welcoming, safe, and equitable for everyone.

We are committed to fostering an environment that respects the dignity, rights, and contributions of all individuals, regardless of race, ethnicity, caste, color, age, physical characteristics, neurodiversity, disability, sex or gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, language, philosophy or religion, national or social origin, socio-economic position, level of education, or other status.

Everyone who participates in good faith and follows this Code of Conduct has the same privileges of participation.

## Encouraged Behaviors

While acknowledging differences in social norms, we strive to meet the project's expectations for positive behavior. Words and actions may be interpreted differently than intended based on culture, background, or native language.

With these considerations in mind, we agree to:

1. Respect the purpose of the Parcelis community, its activities, and its ways of gathering.
2. Engage kindly and honestly with others.
3. Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
4. Take responsibility for our actions and contributions.
5. Gracefully give and accept constructive feedback.
6. Commit to repairing harm when it occurs.
7. Behave in ways that promote and sustain the well-being of the community.

## Restricted Behaviors

We agree to restrict the following behaviors in the Parcelis community. Instances, threats, and promotion of these behaviors are violations of this Code of Conduct.

1. Harassment, including violating clearly expressed boundaries or continuing unnecessary personal attention after a clear request to stop.
2. Character attacks, including insulting, demeaning, or pejorative comments directed at a community member or group.
3. Stereotyping or discrimination based on immutable identities or traits.
4. Sexualization or behavior that would generally be considered inappropriately intimate for the context or purpose of the community.
5. Violating confidentiality by sharing or acting on someone's personal or private information without permission.
6. Endangerment, including causing, encouraging, or threatening violence or other harm toward any person or group.
7. Other behavior that threatens the well-being of the community.

### Other Restrictions

1. Misleading identity, including impersonating someone else or pretending to be someone else to evade enforcement.
2. Failing to credit sources for contributed content.
3. Sharing marketing or commercial content outside the norms of the community.
4. Irresponsibly presenting content that includes, links to, or describes restricted behaviors.

## Reporting an Issue

Tensions can occur even when community members are trying their best to collaborate. Not every conflict represents a Code of Conduct violation, and this Code of Conduct reinforces norms that can help avoid conflict and minimize harm.

When an incident does occur, report it promptly. To report a possible violation, contact the Parcelis maintainers privately at parcelis@callahantech.services.

Community moderators take reports seriously and will make every effort to respond in a timely manner. They may investigate reports by reviewing messages, logs, recordings, or by interviewing witnesses and participants. Moderators will keep investigation and enforcement actions as transparent as possible while prioritizing safety and confidentiality.

Enforcement actions are carried out privately with the involved parties, though communicating to the whole community may be part of a mutually agreed resolution.

## Addressing and Repairing Harm

If an investigation finds that this Code of Conduct has been violated, the following enforcement ladder may be used to determine how best to repair harm, based on the incident's impact on the individuals involved and the community as a whole. Depending on severity, lower steps may be skipped.

1. Warning
   - Event: A violation involving a single incident or series of incidents.
   - Consequence: A private, written warning from community moderators.
   - Repair: Examples include a private written apology, acknowledgement of responsibility, and seeking clarification on expectations.
2. Temporarily Limited Activities
   - Event: A repeated violation after a warning, or the first instance of a more serious violation.
   - Consequence: A private, written warning with a time-limited cooldown period. The cooldown may apply to specific communication channels or interactions with specific community members.
   - Repair: Examples include making an apology, reflecting on actions and impact, and being thoughtful when re-entering community spaces.
3. Temporary Suspension
   - Event: A repeated pattern of violations after warnings, or a single serious violation.
   - Consequence: A private written warning with conditions for return from suspension.
   - Repair: Examples include respecting the suspension, meeting the conditions for return, and thoughtfully reintegrating with the community.
4. Permanent Ban
   - Event: A pattern of repeated violations that other enforcement steps have not resolved, or a violation so serious that moderators determine there is no reasonable way to keep the community safe with this person as a member.
   - Consequence: Access to community spaces, tools, and communication channels is removed.
   - Repair: There is no possible repair in cases of this severity.

This enforcement ladder is a guideline. It does not limit the ability of community moderators to use discretion and judgment in the best interests of the Parcelis community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all Parcelis community spaces. It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public or private spaces, such as using an official project email address, posting through an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.
